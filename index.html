About Geralt of Rivia

Geralt of Rivia is the legendary monster hunter and main character of *The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt*. Known for his white hair, dual swords, and moral complexity, Geralt travels across war-torn lands hunting beasts and searching for his adopted daughter, Ciri. This site explores the game, its story, and the world of The Witcher.
